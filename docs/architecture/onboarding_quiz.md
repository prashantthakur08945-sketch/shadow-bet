# Onboarding Quiz: The Shadow Protocol

These questions are designed to extract the "evil twin" persona from the user.

## Question 1: The Alarm
**Q**: Your alarm goes off at 6:00 AM. What's your move?
- A) Delete the app. I don't follow rules.
- B) Snooze for 2 hours. Life is a suggestion.
- C) Stare at the ceiling and contemplate the void.
- D) Gym. To outrun my demons.

## Question 2: The Group Chat
**Q**: A heated argument breaks out in the main group chat. You:
- A) Drop a 'yikes' and mute for a year.
- B) Send a screenshot to the person they're talking about.
- C) Fuel the fire with a controversial meme.
- D) Type a 5-paragraph essay on why everyone is wrong.

## Question 3: The Ex
**Q**: Your ex texts you "I miss you" at 2:00 AM.
- A) "Who is this?"
- B) Send a LinkedIn connection request.
- C) Reply with a Spotify playlist of songs they hate.
- D) Left on read. Forever.
